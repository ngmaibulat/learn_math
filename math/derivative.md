$$ (fg)' = f'g + fg' $$
$$ (\frac{f}{g})' = (f \cdot \frac{1}{g})' = f' \cdot \frac{1}{g} - f g' \cdot \frac{1}{g^2} $$
$$ (f(g(x)))' = f'(g(x)) \cdot g(x) $$
